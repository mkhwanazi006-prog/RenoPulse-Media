Frequently Asked Questions

How do I request a quote?

Browse our contractor categories or use the search feature to find professionals that match your project. Select a contractor, click "Request a Quote," and complete the project enquiry form. Your request will then be sent directly to the contractor.

Is RenoPulse free?

Yes. RenoPulse is completely free for homeowners looking to find contractors and request quotations.

How are contractors verified?

We review business information, contact details, relevant documentation, and previous work where applicable to maintain a trusted network.

Can I compare multiple quotations?

Absolutely. We encourage homeowners to request and compare quotations from different contractors to help you make an informed decision.

How do reviews work?

After your project has been completed, you'll have the opportunity to leave an honest review based on workmanship, professionalism, and overall satisfaction.

How do I join RenoPulse?

Click on "Join RenoPulse" and complete the contractor registration form with your business details, contact information, services offered, and portfolio.

How much does it cost?

During our launch phase, contractor listings may be offered free of charge. As RenoPulse grows, we plan to introduce affordable subscription packages.

How do I receive leads?

When homeowners submit quote requests within your service area, you'll receive the enquiry directly through your contractor account and registered email.

How do I become verified?

Verification requires completing your profile and submitting business registration details or certifications. Approved profiles receive a Verified badge.

Can I edit my profile?

Yes. Contractors can update services, upload recent project photos, expand service areas, and keep their portfolio current at any time.
